Privacy Policy for WeFin Calculator

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

WeFin Calculator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our WeqaCalc mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear: WeFin Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive user data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Identifiable Information: We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other information that could identify you.
  • Financial Data: All financial calculations and data you input (e.g., loan amounts, interest rates, investment figures, retirement goals) are processed locally on your device only. This data is never sent to our servers, stored externally, or shared with third parties.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect information about how you use the app, such as features accessed, frequency of use, or duration of sessions.
  • Device Information: We do not collect device-specific information such as model, OS version, or identifiers.
  • Location Data: WeqaCalc does not access or track your location.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal or sensitive information, we do not use your information in any way. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

3. Permissions

WeFin Calculator is designed to function without intrusive permissions. It does not request access to:

  • Contacts
  • Camera or microphone
  • Photo gallery or media files
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Storage (beyond what’s needed for installation)
  • Network access (except for app download/updates)

4. Third-Party Services

WeFin Calculator does not integrate with analytics, advertising, or other third-party data collection tools. It offers a private, local experience.

5. Data Security

We do not collect or store user data, but the app itself is built securely. All inputs remain on your device, protected by your system’s security features.

6. Children's Privacy

WeFin Calculator is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. You are encouraged to review it periodically.
